How to Play

  • Both players start with 12 pieces
  • Click on Piece (will be highlighted) then click on tile to move piece
  • Piece only move diagnolly and can only move forward
  • When a piece reaches the end of the board(opponents side) it will be become King
  • King piece(will be different colored) can move back and forth
  • If a piece is next oppents piece, piece can hop over the oppents piece to move diagnolly, and eliminates piece

Objective

  • Remove opppents pieces from the board
